Our Services
OUR COMPANY SERVICES

RICH Innovation Engineering Pte Ltd offers Installation and Maintenance of AIR CONDITIONING & VENTILATION SYSTEM, ELECTRICAL, ELV, FIRE PROTECTION AND PLUMBING & SANITARY SYSTEM.

ACMV SYSTEM

We provide design, installation of ACMV (Air Conditioning and Mechanical Ventilation) System Such as; Split unit, Multi Split, VRV Air Conditioning and Chill Water Piping and Ducting Systems for Commercial and Residential Building. For New construction and A&A works

Electrical & ELV System

We provide design, installation of Electrical system such as; Sub Main Cabling, Bus Bar, Cable support system and Final circuit Wiring works. For New construction and A&A works

Our range of ELV System Service are as follows:

  • Data and Telephone Cabling Installation System
  • Public Address System & CCTV
  • Server and Patch Panel Installation
  • Security and Door Access System
Fire Protection System

We provide design, installation of Fire protection systems. For New construction and A&A works

Our range of Fire Protection System services are as follows:

  • Sprinkler System
  • Gas Suppression System – FM200
  • Hose Reel System
  • Dry and Wet Riser System
  • Fire Alarm System
  • FSSD Submission
Plumbing & Sanitary System

We specialize in Sanitary & plumbing installation for both Commercial and Residential building, ranging from sink, pipe, toilet bowl, tap, water tank, water heater installation, etc. For New construction and A&A works
